Prototype Scissors


Timeline:

1 week

Tools Used:

Pencil & Paper, Woodworking Tools, Foam

This was the first project in the class DESIGN 316: Introduction to Industrial design. We were given one week to reasearch scissors and come up with a) a market to design for, b) competitors in that market, c) possible features for the given niche, and d) a design concept. Then we were shown the woodshop and given high-density foam with which to prototype. And prototype we did! I had a blast.
